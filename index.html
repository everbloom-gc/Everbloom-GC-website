BLOOMorDECAY

Two paths. No third option. You either show up — for the scrim, for the comms call, for the teammate having a rough night — or you don't. That's it.

Everbloom isn't a hashtag. It's a habit. It's the Tuesday VOD review nobody wants to do. The hold you take that doesn't get the kill but wins the round. The DM that says good game when you lost.

We don't promise titles. We promise the room — structured, kind, sharp — where you get to find out what you're actually capable of. The rest is on you.

— RAPH & RUBY · FOUNDERS

GOT QUESTIONS?

FAQ

How can I join Everbloom Gaming?

Fill out our application form via the "JOIN THE BLOOM" button on our website. We review every application and reach out within 48 hours.

What rank do I need to join?

FLINTA Roster: Silver+. Male Roster: Ascendant+. We value attitude and coachability as much as rank.

How can I scrim against Everbloom?

Use the Scrim Request form on our homepage or reach out directly via Discord. We're always open to structured scrim partnerships.

Where is Everbloom Gaming based?

We are based in the DACH region (Germany, Austria, Switzerland), founded in January 2026 by Raph and Ruby.

Are you looking for sponsors or partners?

Yes! Reach out to us via Discord or check our sponsor page. We're open to meaningful partnerships that align with our values.
