Privacy Policy

1. General Information

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. This website is operated by Everbloom Gaming (Ruby Grube, Am Langen Felde 20, 30900 Wedemark, Germany).

2. Data Collection — Application Form

When you submit an application to join one of our rosters, we collect the following data on a voluntary basis:

  • Your first name (real name)
  • Your Discord username & In-Game Tag
  • Your current and peak Valorant rank
  • Your age and previous team experience

This data is used exclusively to evaluate your application and contact you regarding roster decisions. It will not be passed on to third parties.

3. Data Collection — Scrim Request Form

When you submit a scrim request via our website, we collect the following data:

  • Your organization or team name
  • Your average rank
  • Your Discord tag
  • Your preferred match time

This data is transmitted directly to our Discord server via a webhook and is used solely for the purpose of coordinating practice matches. It is not stored on our servers and not passed on to third parties.

4. External Services — Elfsight

Our website uses the Elfsight widget service to display our X (Twitter) feed. When you visit this page, Elfsight may collect technical data such as your IP address and browser information. This is subject to Elfsight's Privacy Policy. We have no influence over data collected by Elfsight.

5. External Services — Google Fonts

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts. When the page is loaded, your browser connects to Google's servers, which may log your IP address. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Purpose of Processing

All data collected via this website is used exclusively for the establishment and management of Everbloom Gaming's esports teams. No data is sold or shared with uninvolved third parties.

7. Data Retention

Application data is stored for the duration of the selection process and deleted thereafter unless you become a member of our team. Scrim request data is not stored beyond the Discord message transmission.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to the processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: everbloomgc@gmail.com
